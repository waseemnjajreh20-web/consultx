S1 = Buildings a maximum of one story above grade plane equipped throughout with an automatic sprinkler system

with an automatic sprinkler system installed in accordance with Section 903.3.1.3 .

903.2.5 .

f. New and existing Group I-2 occupancies are required to be protected by an automatic sprinkler system in accordance 
with Section 903.2.6 and Section 1103.5 of SBC 801.

h. New Group R occupancies are required to be protected by an automatic sprinkler system in accordance with Section 
903.2.8 .

be permitted to comply with Table C102.1of Appendix C in this code.
