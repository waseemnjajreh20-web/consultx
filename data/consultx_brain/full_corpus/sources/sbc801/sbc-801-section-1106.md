# Section 1106 — Requirements for Outdoor Operations /متطلبات العمليات الخارجية

---

## 📋 نص الكود الحرفي / Canonical Code Text
> **[CANONICAL_SOURCE — SBC 801-CC-2024, Section 1106, pp. 5630-5659]**

**1106.1 Tire storage yards.** Existing tire storage yards shall be provided with fire apparatus access roads in accordance with Sections 1106.1.1 and 1106.1.2.

**1106.1.1 Access to piles.** Access roadways shall be within 45 m of any point in the storage yard where storage piles are located not less than 6 m from any storage pile.

**1106.1.2 Location within piles.** Fire apparatus access roads shall be located within all pile clearances identified in Section 3405.4 and within all fire breaks required in Section 3405.5.

## 💡 التعليق الهندسي / Engineering Commentary
> **[LLM_SYNTHESIS]**

هذا القسم يقدم متطلبات الوصول لمركبات الإطفاء في ساحات تخزين الإطارات القائمة. يوفر متطلبات الوصول التي تضمن قدرة مركبات الإطفاء على الوصول إلى any point within the storage yard.

The requirements ensure fire apparatus access roads are within specific distances from storage piles and within required fire breaks.
