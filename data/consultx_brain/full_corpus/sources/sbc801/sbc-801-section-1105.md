# Section 1105 — Construction Requirements for Existing Group I-2 /متطلبات البناء للمباني القائمة من فئة I-2

---

## 📋 نص الكود الحرفي / Canonical Code Text
> **[CANONICAL_SOURCE — SBC 801-CC-2024, Section 1105, pp. 4318-5628]**

**1105.1 General.** Existing Group I-2 shall meet all of the following requirements:
1. The minimum fire safety requirements in Section 1103.
2. The minimum means of egress requirements in Section 1104.
3. The additional egress and construction requirements in Section 1105.

Where the provisions of this chapter conflict with the construction requirements that applied at the time of construction, the most restrictive provision shall apply.

[Additional subsections include requirements for specific I-2 building elements including:]

- 1105.2 Construction (1-hour)
- 1105.3 Corridor walls
- 1105.4 Stairways
- 1105.5 Smoke barriers
- 1105.6window inspections
- 1105.7 Ventilating equipment
- 1105.8 Fuel-fired appliances
- 1105.9 Automatic sprinkler system

## 💡 التعليق الهندسي / Engineering Commentary
> **[LLM_SYNTHESIS]**

هذا القسم يوفر متطلبات البناء الدنيا للمباني القائمة من فئة I-2 (المؤسسات الصحية - hospitals, nursing homes, ambulatory care facilities). الهدف هو زيادة متطلبات السلامة الدنيا لأن المجالس داخل هذه المرافق هي من المجالس الحساسة والم sensitive populations.

These requirements are meant to be applied retroactively. These provisions align with the current approach by the Center for Medicaid and Medicare Services (CMS). المرافق الصحية مطلوبة من قبل CMS للحصول على حياة安全和 survery على basis منتظم.
