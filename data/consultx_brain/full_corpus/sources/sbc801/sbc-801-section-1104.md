# Section 1104 — Means of Egress for Existing Buildings /مسارات الإخلاء للمباني القائمة

---

## 📋 نص الكود الحرفي / Canonical Code Text
> **[CANONICAL_SOURCE — SBC 801-CC-2024, Section 1104, pp. 3168-4315]**

**1104.1 General.** Means of egress in existing buildings shall comply with the minimum egress requirements where specified in Table 1103.1 as further enumerated in Sections 1104.2 through 1104.25, and the building code that applied at the time of construction. Where the provisions of this chapter conflict with the building code that applied at the time of construction, the most restrictive provision shall apply. Existing buildings that were not required to comply with a building code at the time of construction shall comply with the minimum egress requirements where specified in Table 1103.1 as further enumerated in Sections 1104.2 through 1104.25.

**1104.2 Elevators, escalators and moving walks.** Elevators, escalators and moving walks shall not be used as a component of a required means of egress.

*Exceptions:*
1. Elevators used as an accessible means of egress where allowed by Section 1009.4.
2. Previously approved elevators, escalators and moving walks in existing buildings.

**1104.3 Exit sign illumination.** Exit signs shall be illuminated as required.

**1104.4 Power source.** The power source for exit signs shall comply with this section.

**1104.5 Illumination emergency power.** Where illumination is required for exit signs, emergency power shall be provided.

**1104.6 Guards.** Guards shall comply with this section.

**1104.7 Size of doors.** The required capacity of means of egress doors shall be sized as specified.

**1104.8 Opening force for doors.** The opening force for doors shall comply with this section.

**1104.9 Revolving doors.** Revolving doors shall comply with this section.

## 💡 التعليق الهندسي / Engineering Commentary
> **[LLM_SYNTHESIS]**

هذا القسم يوفر متطلبات مسارات الإخلاء الدنيا للمباني القائمة. المتطلبات المقصودة هي require existing buildings to comply with specific means of egress requirements for new buildings except as modified by this section.

where an item is specifically addressed by Section 1104, the requirements of this section allow existing buildings to not fully meet the requirements for new buildings in Sections 1003 through 1031.

The primary concept is to allow existing buildings to not fully meet new building requirements while still providing minimum life safety.
