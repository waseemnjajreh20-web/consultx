# Section 308.1.6.3

## Canonical Code Text

308.1.6.3 
Sky lanterns. A person shall not 
release or cause to be released an untethered sky 
lantern. 
❖ 
Sky lanterns, variously known as Kongming 
lanterns, Chinese lanterns, fire balloons or fire 
parachutes, are typically made of paper and 
contain an open flame used to heat the air 
inside the device to make it airborne, similar to 
but on a much smaller scale than a hot air 
balloon. Once airborne, if these devices are 
untethered and their movement is thus 
unrestricted, they are subject to winds and 
other atmospheric conditions such that the 
location of the final landfall is completely 
unknown and uncontrolled by the user. 
Obviously, uncontrolled open flame devices 
descending out of the sky have a significant 
potential to start wildfires and structure fires.
