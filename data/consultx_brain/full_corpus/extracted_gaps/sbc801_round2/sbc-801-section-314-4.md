# Section 314.4

## Canonical Code Text

314.4 Vehicles. Liquid or gas-fueled vehicles, 
boats or other motorcraft shall not be located 
indoors except as follows: 
1. The engine starting system is made 
inoperable or batteries are disconnected 
except where the Fire official requires that 
the batteries remain connected to maintain 
safety features. 
2. Fuel in fuel tanks does not exceed one-
quarter tank or 19 L (whichever is least). 
3. Fuel tanks and fill openings are closed and 
sealed to prevent tampering. 
4. Vehicles, 
boats 
or 
other 
motorcraft 
equipment are not fueled or defueled within 
the building.
