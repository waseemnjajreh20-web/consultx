# Section 904.7

## Canonical Code Text

904.7   Foam  
systems.   
Foam-extinguishing  
systems shall be installed, maintained, periodically  
inspected and tested in accordance with NFPA 11  
and NFPA 16 and their listing. Records of  
inspections and testing shall be maintained.

904.7.1   System test.  Foam-extinguishing systems  
shall be inspected and tested at intervals in  
accordance with NFPA 25.
