# Section 105.3.5

## Canonical Code Text

105.3.5 Posting the permit. Issued permits shall be 
kept on the premises designated therein at all times 
and shall be readily available for inspection by the 
Fire official. 
❖ 
Note that this section does not require the 
permit to be posted, but it is to be kept on site 
at all times for inspection by the Fire official.
