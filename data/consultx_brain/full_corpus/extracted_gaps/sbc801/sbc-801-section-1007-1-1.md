# Section 1007.1.1

## Canonical Code Text

1007.1.1 
Two 
exits 
or 
exit 
access 
doorways. Where two exits, exit access doorways, 
exit access stairways or ramps, or any combination 
thereof, are required from any portion of the exit 
access, they shall be placed a distance apart equal to 
not less than one-half of the length of the maximum 
overall diagonal dimension of the building or area 
to be served measured in a straight line between 
them. Interlocking or scissor stairways shall be 
counted as one exit stairway. 
Exceptions: 
1. Where interior exit stairways or ramps are 
interconnected by a 1-hour fire-resistance-
rated 
corridor 
conforming 
to 
the 
requirements of Section 1020, the required 
exit separation shall be measured along the 
shortest direct line of travel within the 
corridor.
