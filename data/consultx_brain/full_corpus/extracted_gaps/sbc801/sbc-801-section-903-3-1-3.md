# Section 903.3.1.3

## Canonical Code Text

903.3.1.3   
NFPA 13D sprinkler systems.   
Automatic sprinkler systems installed in one- and  
two-family dwellings; Group R-3; Group R-4,  
Condition 1; and townhouses shall be permitted to  
be installed throughout in accordance with NFPA  
13D .
