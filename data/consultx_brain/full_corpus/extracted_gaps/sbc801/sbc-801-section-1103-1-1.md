# Section 1103.1.1

## Canonical Code Text

1103.1.1, which provides some minimal 
requirements. Section 1103.1.1 requires a 
plan to be developed in accordance with NFPA 
914. The reference to NFPA 914 provides 
specific guidance to building and fire officials, 
design professionals and building owners as to 
the provisions that are applicable to fire 
protection plans for historic buildings in order to 
provide a reasonable level of building and life 
safety provisions.
